Waffle

These waffles turn out lovely and crispy.

Perfect for any day of the week!

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1.5 cups warm milk
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  1. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter and vanilla.
  3. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.
  4. Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron.
  5. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
